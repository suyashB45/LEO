Our Activities

Discover the diverse range of activities and programs we organize

Meeting

Gram Sabha Meeting

Engaging with local governance and raising voices through impactful rallies. Fostering student responsibility in building informed, healthy, and socially aware communities.

50+ Students Helped Weekly Sessions
Campaign

Importance of Waste Segregation

Creating cleaner communities by educating households on smart waste segregation. Promoting sustainable living through simple habits that protect our planet.

30+ Students Annual
Rally

Yellow Bag Rally

Spreading awareness through vibrant village rallies against plastic pollution. Promoting eco-friendly cloth bags and sustainable habits for a cleaner tomorrow.

30+ Students Monthly
Training

Disaster Management and Prevention Training

Building a safer village through awareness on disaster readiness and emergency response. Empowering communities with life-saving skills in fire safety, first aid, and evacuation.

Campus-wide Monthly
Health

Medical Camp

Offering free health checkups to ensure early detection and well-being in rural communities. From eye care to sugar tests, promoting healthier lives through accessible medical support.

40+ students Quarterly
Survey

Household Survey

Surveying rural households to understand their living conditions, health, and social needs. Gathering insights to drive impactful solutions for a better quality of life in the village.

30+ Students Bi-annual
Community

Wheel Chair Issuance Program

Empowering lives through care—wheelchair support extended to Mr. K. Sarangan for improved mobility. A step toward dignity and independence, reflecting Leo Club's dedication to community well-being.

100+ Chairs Issued Annual
